Amazing War Machines

VOLS buggies are perfect both for assault and defense tactics. They are a prominent choice for snipers, medics, and guerilla warfare. Let’s take a closer look at these war machines.

Optimum Power Reserve

A unique-shaped 38-liter gas tank and a small combustion engine give over 500 kilometers of operative range without refueling.

Well-secured

Armored seats of VOLS buggy are able to withstand a 7.62 bullet shot and provide reliable head and back protection.

Easy Service

The accessible engine makes it easy to repair buggies if needed.

Wide range of functions

Each VOLS buggy accommodates machine gun and grenade launchers; Each one has a separate section to evacuate the wounded soldiers away from firing lines.
